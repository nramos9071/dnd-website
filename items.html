Potions and Buff/Debuff Items

  • Healing Salve - Heals 1d4 damage once applied.
  • Greater Healing Salve - Heals 2d4 damage once applied.
  • Rations - Gives the equivalent of a short rest using 2 Hit Die to however consumes it.
  • Greater Rations - Gives the user the equivalent of a short rest using full Hit Die without expending any hit die.
  • Tea - Cures the drinker from the poisoned condition.
  • Refined Tea - Cures the drinker from the poisoned condition and heals
  • Owlbear Steaks - This explains how to make Owlbear steaks. Owlbear steaks give +1 to strength ability checks and saving throws. The steaks last for 1d4 days before going bad. Effects last until the next short/long rest.
