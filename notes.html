  • Session 1

    I met Arc as he journeyed through the woods. We saw a ship crash in the distance and decided to check for survivors and/or threats. There were dead Xaraxian guards & Barrork was fighting a Goliath. We decided to jump in and help. Arc turned into a fucking spider! After the threat was eliminated we investigated a sphinx with a skull & book in its hands. It was rigged to kill anyone who tried to take the stuff with lightning damage, but we found a way using sticks to push it all off. We touched the stuff and we were transported to wildspace outside of Toril. We saw a group of people floating/standing over a person kneeling. Barrork walked closer to the group to listen. Caiphon is the one kneeling. People around him are asking him to repent for something he’s done in order for power. Caiphon wanted mortals to worship him and the others as gods. They bind Caiphon to the mortal world (Toril) to be judged. They all begin to chant, except for one female named Gibith, but she eventually joins in. Caiphon stretched and pinned down to Toril. We suddenly returned to the temple. The skull speaks to us, saying he is Mimir and here to help us decide Caiphpon’s judgement.

Barrork attempted to talk to them, but was attacked. So we ran and they didn’t chase us. Maybe they're guarding something? Arc asked the nature around the area but they didn't know shit. We decided to go back to Mathieu.
