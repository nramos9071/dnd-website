Budding Prospects: The Beginners Guide into Herbology

By: Cheech Morin

The book gives a brief introduction into the science of herbology

Towards the back of the book you find a section of recipes

Recipes

Healing Salve

Heals user 1d4 HP

  • 2x Aloe Vera
  • 1x Lavender
  • 1x Honey

Rations

Gives the user the equivalent of a short rest using 2 Hit Die

  • 1x Dried Meat
  • 1x Ginseng

Tea

Cooking on the Run: Easy Recipes for Cooking in the Wild

By: Ray'Chill Rae

This book gives detailed information on cooking and can be used to learn how to use cooking utensils. After the instructional section, there are recipes for food in the wild.

Recipes

Dire Wolf Steaks

This explains how to make Dire Wolf Steaks. Dire Wolf steaks give a +2 to attack bonus when consumed. The steaks last for 1d4 days before going bad. Effects last until the next short/long rest.

  • 1x Dire Wolf Meat

Owl Steaks

This explains how to make Owlbear steaks. Owlbear steaks give +1 to strength ability checks and saving throws. The steaks last for 1d4 days before going bad. Effects last until the next short/long rest.

  • 1x Wood

Edible Edibles: Intermediate guide into Herbal Remedies

By: Jessica Mckown

This book is a more advanced book in herbology. There is some explanation, but very quickly it begins to say terms that the layman doesn't know. If someone unaware of how to use Herbalism Tools tried to use this book they would be lost.



Towards the back of the book you find a section of recipes but use terms hard to understand.

Recipes

Greater Healing Salve

Heals user 2d4 HP

  • 2x Aloe Vera
  • 1x Bee Wax
  • 1x Lavender
  • 1x Honey

Greater Rations

Gives the user the equivalent of a short rest using full Hit Die without expending any.

  • 4x Owlbear Dried Meat
  • 1x Ginseng

Refined Tea

Cures the drinker from the poisoned condition and gives 1d4 health back

  • 4x Sage
  • 1x Honey
